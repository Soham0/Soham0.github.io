I am a Data Scientist at Flipkart Internet Private Limited, India. Previously, I was a Research Assistant at the Center for Visual Information Technology at IIIT Hyderabad, India. I pursued my MS by Research there under the guidence of Prof. C.V.Jawahar and Dr. Girish Varma.

My areas of research were Efficient Neural Network architectures for Computer Vision tasks. More recently, I have developed interest in Explainable AI and Perceiving Neural Networks, which lie in the intersection of Deep Learning and Computer Vision.
